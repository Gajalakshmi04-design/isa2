Elixir

A spellbinding magical drink restaurant where the art of mixology meets the mystique of witchcraft. Each drink here is a unique potion waiting to be concoted.

Elixir, reaches out to people wanting to break free from their normal routine to experience the magic it gives, where every mistake creates art and happiness For this project, I delved deeply into the history of witchcraft and wizardry. In the process of creating the logo, I incorporated various historical symbols and occult-related details. The brand embodies the magician archetype, and to attract customers, I plan to use hashtags and photographs strategically.
